Getting your food

Delivery & Info

Everything you need to know about how we get HUTKO frozen food from our kitchen to your door — anywhere in the Netherlands.

The process

How delivery works

1
🛒
Place your order

Browse our shop, pick your favourite dishes, choose your portion sizes and add them to the cart.

2
✅
Order confirmed

You'll receive a confirmation via email or WhatsApp within 1 hour of placing your order.

3
❄️
Packed & frozen

Your food is carefully packed in insulated boxes to stay frozen throughout the entire delivery journey.

4
🚚
At your door

Your order arrives fresh-frozen. Store in the freezer and cook when you're ready — it's that simple.

📦
Minimum order
Minimum order value is €25. Orders below this amount are not eligible for delivery.
🚚
Delivery cost
Flat rate €5 delivery. Free delivery on orders over €60 anywhere in the Netherlands.
📅
Order cutoff
Orders placed before 14:00 are processed the same day. Later orders are processed next morning.
