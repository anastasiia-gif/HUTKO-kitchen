Terms & Conditions

1. Service
HUTKO Kitchen provides frozen Ukrainian food delivery across the Netherlands. Orders are accepted online and delivered on Thursdays and Saturdays.

2. Orders & Payment
Orders must be paid via bank transfer or cash on delivery within 24 hours of confirmation. We reserve the right to cancel unpaid orders.

3. Delivery
We deliver across all Netherlands provinces. Minimum order €25. Flat rate €5 delivery, free over €60. Delivery times may vary.

4. Food Safety
All products are frozen immediately after cooking. Store at -18°C. Follow cooking instructions on packaging. Not suitable for refreezing after thawing.

5. Cancellations
Orders may be cancelled up to 24 hours before the delivery date. Contact us at orders@hutko-kitchen.com.

6. Data
We process your personal data (name, address, email) solely to fulfill your orders. We do not share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy.
